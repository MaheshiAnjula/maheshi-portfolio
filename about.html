About Me

I'm an undergraduate student pursuing a B.Sc. in Health Promotion at the Faculty of Applied Sciences, Rajarata University of Sri Lanka. With a deep passion for public health and community well-being, I strive to create meaningful change through both academic knowledge and hands-on experience.

As a Health Promotion Facilitator, I work closely with a rural community in Mihintale, leading initiatives that empower individuals and foster healthier lifestyles. This role has allowed me to grow as a communicator, decision-maker, and public speaker while strengthening my language and leadership skills.

Beyond academics, I'm a dedicated volunteer and an active member of the ZeroPlastic community at Rajarata University. I’ve contributed to impactful environmental projects and taken on leadership roles within organizing committees—experiences that continue to fuel my passion for sustainable development and collaborative action.
